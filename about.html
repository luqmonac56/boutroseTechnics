About us

Few words about us

Boutrose Technics is a registered Limited Liability company that delivers a wide range of services which includes Project Management, Construction Management, Building Construction Civil and Structural Engineering, Construction Management, Highway Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Jetty and Pile Foundation, and Mechanical & Electrical Works. We also offer a design and Build Package to suit the specific needs of our Clients.

Our record of customer satisfaction will continue to grow, as we have recently expanded our ability to service our client’s needs by providing quality Construction and Supply chain management (SCM), Health, Safety and Environment, Site development, Project Management and Design/Build services.

We have a team of qualified and seasoned professionals, with several years of experience in construction industry. We guarantee a quality and safe service delivery. As a service organization, we strive to meet our service guarantees as well as our client expectation. We deliver promptly and efficiently. With a team of young, visionary and resourceful engineers, Boutrose is building a proud history and strategizing his market positioning in government and infrastructure sectors by being a low-cost, high-efficiency and absolutely reliable service provider.

One of our company’s strongest personality traits is commitment. We are committed to our clients, our team members, and our industry and to each other. Commitment is a trait that all the best team and their members possess.

Our mission

Our main objective is building an outfit that relates with its client by rendering quality and affordable services anchored on integrity and sound professional ethics and principles, both at the design and execution stages of all projects.

We, as an organization, are ready to stand out as a reference point in the construction industry. This we will do by rendering quality services that will satisfy our clients‟ aspirations- economically, socially and otherwise. We are also building an outfit that will evolve an efficient and low cost approach to project development and management with strict adherence and compliance to laid down professional ethics and procedures both in the design and execution stages of every project.

Our vision

If construction were easy, there will be no need for a company like Boutrose Technics. Today and tomorrow, we work with our clients wherever they are to bring construction to the world.

We will continue to contribute to their success, through our constant customer focus and our integrated and sustainable project approach. As the industry reference, we will demonstrate the knowhow, the commitment and the inspiration to help all of our partners push further to achieve their goals. This is our vision and above all, it is our passion.
