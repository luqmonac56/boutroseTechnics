Our services

Geotechnical Engineering and Pile Foundation

Boutrose Geotechnical and marine services involve In-situ and Lab Soil Testing, piling, basement constructions, shoreline protection and generally more complex structures. Restricted site services involve working underneath, within or beside an existing structure.

Architecture and Structural Engineering

At Boutrose, we are picky about details of your construction project. In fact, our reputation for excellent construction and unique residential additions has made us the choice of every best architect who insists on the highest level of attention to details. We offer a level of construction excellence that is seldom seen in the industry.

Renovation and maintenance of existing building

We are established and experienced maintenance, construction, renovation and Repair Company specializing in providing all the services required for our clients. We pride ourselves in providing a high standard of workmanship at reasonable prices to ensure our customers get the best value from our services.

Landscaping and Environmental Services

The broad range of professional skills and services we offer is a key feature of the business. As such we can deliver a greater depth and breadth of expertise that can deliver better results and a hassle free construction programme for both the consultant and client. Our services include – project budgeting, estimation, project management, all aspects of landscape construction, minor civil works, revegetation works, rehabilitation works, wetland construction and maintenance.

Building Construction

BOUTROSE TECHNICS LIMITED currently provides the following building core services: Building Surveying Consultancy, Building Surveying Projects, Cost Consultancy, Project Management. Its scope of work covers the whole spectrum of building and construction consultancy, technical, due diligence, management, compliance and also incorporate a wide range of building types: commercial, industrial, retail, residential, public and historic.

Project Management

Planning a construction project takes the consolidated effort of several professionals, each with varying experiences, knowledge and expectations. We capture the vision of these parties and unite them as integrated team. Boutrose is committed to understanding the needs and expectations of all the players in the process and dedicated to creating an approach that allows all team members to succeed.

Highway road and drainages

Boutrose is well experienced in road and drainage construction and generally in all aspect of building and civil engineering works: these include:

  • Building roads, highways and bridges construction.
  • Flood/erosion controls/culverts and drainage structures.
  • Water supply both surface and sub-surface.
  • Coastal structure including domestic, commercial and still structures.
  • Renovation and maintenance works in both civil and building engineering.
  • Construction of septic tank and evacuation of sludge.
